About Pine Hills Grammar School

Welcome to Pine Hills Grammar School, a prestigious educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering academic excellence.

Our Mission

At Pine Hills Grammar School, our mission is to nurture the intellectual, social, and emotional development of our students, preparing them to become responsible global citizens and lifelong learners.

About the Mathematics Department

The Mathematics Department at Pine Hills Grammar School is committed to inspiring a love for mathematics and developing strong mathematical skills in our students. Our experienced faculty members utilize innovative teaching methods and cutting-edge resources to create engaging learning experiences that challenge and motivate students.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering a wide range of mathematical topics
  • Interactive and collaborative learning environment
  • Dedicated faculty members with expertise in various areas of mathematics
  • Opportunities for students to participate in math competitions and enrichment activities
